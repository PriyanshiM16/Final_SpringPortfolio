Infy App Chllange (Pencil & Infosys)

The Infy App Challenge by Infosys is a competition where students create an app idea or prototype that solves a real-world problem using technology.
Teams work together to design something useful, like an app for education, health, or everyday life, and then present it to judges.
Being a finalist meant our team’s idea stood out from many others because it was creative, practical, and well thought out, and we were selected to move on to the final round of the challenge hosted by Infosys.
